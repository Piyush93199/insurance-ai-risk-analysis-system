## Key Findings

### Customer Age

* Customers are distributed across a broad age range.
* Age appears to influence insurance costs and risk levels.

### BMI

* Most BMI values fall within the normal to overweight range.
* A small number of high-BMI outliers were observed.

### Insurance Charges

* Insurance charges are positively skewed.
* A limited number of customers incur significantly higher costs.

### Smoking Status

* Smokers have substantially higher insurance charges than non-smokers.
* Smoking status emerged as one of the strongest indicators of insurance risk.

### Gender

* Gender showed relatively limited impact on insurance charges compared to other variables.
