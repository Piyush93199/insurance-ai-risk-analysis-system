# Day 1 Research Notes

## Insurance Domain Overview

Insurance is a financial service that provides protection against potential financial losses. Customers pay premiums to insurance providers in exchange for coverage against specified risks.

The insurance industry relies heavily on customer data, risk assessment, and predictive analysis to determine suitable policies and pricing structures.

## Types of Insurance

### Life Insurance

Provides financial support to beneficiaries upon the policyholder's death.

### Health Insurance

Covers medical expenses and healthcare-related costs.

### Vehicle Insurance

Protects against losses related to automobiles and accidents.

### Property Insurance

Provides coverage for residential and commercial properties.

### Term Insurance

Offers coverage for a specified period at affordable premiums.

## Customer Risk Factors

Common factors used in risk assessment include:

* Age
* Occupation
* Income Level
* Marital Status
* Existing Medical Conditions
* Lifestyle Habits
* Claim History
* Policy History

These factors influence risk classification and policy recommendations.

## Machine Learning Applications in Insurance

### Risk Prediction

Predicting customer risk categories using historical data.

### Fraud Detection

Identifying suspicious claims and fraudulent activities.

### Customer Segmentation

Grouping customers based on characteristics and behavior.

### Recommendation Systems

Suggesting appropriate insurance products based on customer profiles.

### Business Analytics

Providing insights for decision-making through data visualization and predictive analysis.

## Key Learning

Machine learning can significantly improve insurance operations by automating risk assessment, enhancing customer targeting, and supporting data-driven business decisions.
